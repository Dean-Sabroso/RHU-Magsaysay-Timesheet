RHU Magsaysay Timesheet 🕒
A comprehensive and responsive web-based timesheet tracking system designed to help On-the-Job Training (OJT) students effortlessly log their hours, track their progress, and predict their completion date at the Rural Health Unit (RHU).

Features ✨
👤 Intern Profile Management
  -  Upload 2x2 Profile Photo
  - View and Update Personal Details (Name, Year Level)
  - View and Update Deployment Details (Company, Address)

🎯 Goal & Progress Tracking
  - Select Program and Required Hours (e.g., CS 300hrs, IT 280hrs)
  - Real-time "Rendered Hours" Calculation
  - Real-time "Remaining Hours" Tracking
  - Dynamic "Predicted Finish Date" Engine based on average daily hours

⏱️ Time Logging & Management
  - Daily AM and PM Shift Logging (In/Out)
  - "Quick 8-5 Shift" Autofill Button
  - Responsive Records Data Table
  - Edit Modal for Updating Existing Records
  - Delete Individual Records or Reset All Data

📥 Export & Reporting
  - Download Records as CSV
  - Download Records as PDF Report
